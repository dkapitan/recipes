Grilled cola chicken

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 250 ml cola
  • 80 ml ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Â½ teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 6 pieces boneless skinless chicken breast halves
  1. Preheat the grill for medium-high heat.
  2. In a medium saucepan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and saute for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant. Stir in the cola, ketchup, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce and chili powder.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 15 minutes or until sauce reduces and thickens. Remove 6 tablespoons of sauce and set aside.
  4. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, paprika, salt, pepper, and cumin. Rub the spice mixture over the chicken. Place chicken on grill and baste with 3 tablespoons of the reserved sauce.
  5. Cook for 5 minutes, then turn chicken over and baste with the remaining 3 tablespoons of reserved sauce. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Serve with remaining sauce.

Also works nicely with whole chicken. To reduce cooking time, cut chicken in half and flatten.
